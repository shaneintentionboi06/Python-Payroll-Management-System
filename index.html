Automated Salary Computation

The system automates complex financial calculations. Using helper functions like Calc_PF and inc_Tax, it derives the Net Salary from the Basic Salary by applying statutory deductions.

1. Basic Salary

The base pay input for the employee.

2. Deductions

Provident Fund (PF), Professional Tax, and Income Tax are automatically calculated.

3. Net Salary

The final take-home pay after all subtractions.

Real-time Attendance

Managers can mark employee attendance directly through the TUI. The system logs timestamps and statuses (Present/Absent/Late), enabling the generation of attendance reports and calculation of payable days.
